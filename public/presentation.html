Three Roles

Employee Submits claims, receipts, and mileage via WhatsApp or Dashboard.

Views own claims and approval status. Cannot approve or create payments.
Admin Submits claims + invoices. Reviews claims (marks as reviewed).

Manages employees, categories, suppliers, bank statements. Cannot approve claims.
Accountant Approves / rejects claims. Creates payments. Links receipts to invoices.

Manages bank recon, aging reports, SOA. Full financial control.
